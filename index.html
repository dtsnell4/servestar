Customer Ratings Provide Feedback On The Server's Quality Of Service

Customers rate servers based on a wide variety categories.

When looking for the server that meets their needs, they can easily see the server's rating and read other customers' reviews. They can also rate other customers' reviews based on the helpfulness of the review.

These customer rating aggregate to the server's "ServeStar Rating". That's how cusotmers... and potential employers... Can instantly recognize their level of customer service.
